About Perihelion-Aphelion Archive

What is this?

Periaphe (peh·ruh·fee) Archive is a fan-made, comprehensive database managed by Alathea. The purpose of this archive is to track the lives, events, and relationships of DC Comics characters across every continuity and era.

Why?

DC Comics has undergone multiple reboots and timeline restructurings over the decades. This archive serves as a visual, interactive reference for fans who want to explore character evolution.
