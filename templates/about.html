About Perihelion-Aphelion Archive

Periaphe (peh·ruh·fee) Archive is a comprehensive database managed solely by Alathea, a fanwriter with a long-standing fascination for DC Comics' mythos. This archive serves as a visual, interactive reference for fans, or anyone, who want to explore character evolution.

The Eras

Classic

The original DC timeline spanning decades of publishing history.

Post-Crisis (1985)

Following the Crisis on Infinite Earths, a unified timeline emerged.

Flashpoint (2011)

A reality-shattering event where Barry Allen's time travel creates a dark alternate world, leading directly to the New 52.

New 52 (2011)

A bold reboot that reset the universe, modernizing characters and origins.

DC You (2015)

A brief experimental initiative emphasizing diverse voices and genres before merging into Rebirth.

Rebirth (2016)

A return to legacy and hope, restoring connections lost in the New 52 while keeping its modern tone.

New Justice (2018)

The Source Wall breaks. Justice League confronts cosmic threats while Dark Nights: Metal unleashes the Dark Multiverse.

Infinite Frontier (2021)

Embracing the omniverse. All stories matter, all timelines are valid.

All In (2024)

The Absolute Universe debuts. Heroes reimagined without their core advantages alongside mainline continuity in a dual-track publishing initiative.

Elseworlds

Non-canon alternate reality tales exploring "what if" scenarios.

Future State

Dystopian glimpses into potential futures of the DCU.

Black Label
