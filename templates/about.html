About Project Aphelion

What is this?

Project Aphelion is a comprehensive database managed by Alathea. The purpose of this archive is to track the lives, events, and relationships of DC Comics characters (mainly Damian) across every continuity and era.

Why?

DC Comics has undergone multiple reboots and timeline restructurings over the decades. This archive serves as a visual, interactive reference for fans who want to explore character development and continuity evolution.

The Eras

Pre-New 52 (aka Classic)

The original DC timeline spanning decades of publishing history.

Post-Crisis (1985-2011)

Following the Crisis on Infinite Earths, a unified timeline emerged.

New 52 (2011)

A bold reboot that reset the universe, modernizing characters and origins.

Rebirth (2016)

Restoration of legacy and hope, blending classic elements with new stories.

Infinite Frontier (2021)

Embracing the omniverse. All stories matter, all timelines are valid.

Elseworlds

Non-canon alternate reality tales exploring "what if" scenarios.

Future State

Dystopian glimpses into potential futures of the DC Universe.
